Frequently Asked Questions

How much does stump grinding cost in Cleveland?

Stump grinding costs vary based on stump size, quantity, and accessibility. Most residential stumps in the Cleveland area range from $100–$400. Call or text (440) 429-4524 for a free estimate.

How long does stump grinding take?

Most single stumps take 30–60 minutes to grind. Larger stumps or multiple stumps on the same property may take a few hours. We can usually schedule your job within the same week.

What happens to the wood chips after grinding?

Stump grindings are left neatly in the hole for natural backfill, or raked out across the surrounding area. Your yard is left clean and ready for sod, a garden, or a new tree.
